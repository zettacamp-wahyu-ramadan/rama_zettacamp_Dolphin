Avenged Sevenfold

Description

   Avenged Sevenfold is the fourth album by the American band Avenged Sevenfold. The album was released on October 26, 2007. Originally it was intended to be released on October 16, 2007, but was delayed two weeks to complete bonus material and production, including animation for the song "A Little Piece of Heaven". This album occupied the album chart number 4 on the Billboard 200. On September 23, 2008, this album received a gold certificate from the RIAA. The album was supported by a tour, which began the day before the album's release and ended in 2009. The album is a full-length studio album produced prior to the death of Jimmy "The Rev" Sullivan on December 28, 2009, prior to the recording of Nightmare. Although reception to the album was mixed compared to previous releases, Avenged Sevenfold won a Kerrang! as the best album of 2008.
