What is OKR?

In simple terms the acronym stands for
“Objective and Key Results”.

Objective (O): (the O in OKR) are qualitative, inspirational, time-bound goals to
be executed by a team (say, Recruitment) or an individual. Clear
and Easy to understand statement of a goal.
Key result (KR): (the KR in OKR) quantifies the Objective and breaks it into
specific metrics that can be used to measure the achievement of
the desired Objectives.
